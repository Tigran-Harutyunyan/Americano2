About us

Welcome to Prospect Medical Group, an independent physician association (IPA) managed by Prospect Medical Systems. Our network consists of highly qualified primary care and specialty physicians, and we are affiliated with the best hospitals in Orange County.

With Prospect Medical Group, you have access to more than 600 primary care physicians and more than 1,000 specialists. You’re sure to find the doctors that are right for you.

Prospect Medical Group has a long history as a respected leader in managed healthcare. For three decades, we have provided quality care and outstanding service to our members.

We support the use of advanced diagnostic and treatment tools to provide our members with convenient access to state-of-the-art healthcare. Our pledge to members is that we will give you exceptional care delivered by compassionate physicians, who have a strong commitment to each and every patient.

Our physicians work closely with teams of skilled nurses and other healthcare professionals.

Our focus is on your health and well-being.

All of our support services, including care management, referrals for specialty care, customer service, and health education resources are handled by our management service organization, Prospect Medical Systems.

This support gives our doctors the freedom to do what they do best: care for you.

Prospect Medical Group contracts with most major health plans, and we serve all product lines, including commercial, Medicare, Medicaid, Medicare/Medicaid (Medi-Medi) and Health Insurance Exchange.

To further improve the health of the communities we serve, we also offer comprehensive health education classes and support services through our numerous outreach programs.

History

Prospect Medical Group was formed in 1985—thanks to a small group of physicians in Northern Orange County who shared a passion for delivering quality healthcare. What was true 30 years ago remains just as true today: Prospect places an emphasis on delivering exceptional medical care, helps members get maximum value from their insurance coverage, and offers exclusive services to its members.

In 1996, Prospect Medical Systems, the group’s management service organization, was created to develop, implement and manage a full range of services designed to simplify the relationship between doctor and patient. That same year, Prospect Medical Holdings, Inc. was formed as the parent company of Prospect Medical Systems. This has enabled Prospect Medical Group to grow the number of providers and services that we offer our patients.

Today, Prospect Medical Group is made up of more than 600 primary care physicians and more than 1,000 specialists, and is affiliated with some of the best hospitals in Orange County.
